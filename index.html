Side Effect

Medicines can have various side effects, which are unintended and often unwanted effects that occur alongside the desired therapeutic effects. It's important to note that not all individuals will experience side effects, and the severity and occurrence of side effects can vary from person to person. Additionally, different medications have different side effect profiles
